Abstract

1/3 of food produced globally is wasted while many face food shortages. This system helps donors give excess food that NGOs can collect and distribute efficiently.

Proposed System

An interface connecting Donors, NGOs/Admins, and Delivery volunteers to list, claim, and deliver food donations.

User (Donor)

  • Register / Login
  • Create a donation (type, quantity, time)
  • Track status & history

Admin (NGO)

  • See incoming donations
  • Approve & assign pickups
  • Maintain records

Delivery

  • View assigned pickups
  • Map pickup & drop
  • Mark delivered
